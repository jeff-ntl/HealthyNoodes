Our Healthy Noodes

Chicken

The Story

The most beloved flavour in the nation, recreated to perfection with our team of nutritionalists, to give you the flvour you love with added health benefits.

The Recipe

A bowl of Healthy Noodes with garlic chicken and a side of vegetables. Add some butter to a pan and when heated add the chicken. Put some water in a pot and add the noodes plus 3 crushed cloves of garlic. When noodes are soft, add the chicken flavouring and stir in. When noodes are boiling add to the pan with the chicken. Cook until chicken is fully cooked. Serve on some nice plates to impress your roommates.

Beef

The Story

In the country of cows, we felt it only right to add a beef flavour. A favourite of the agriculture students and workers alike. No matter what you do, or where you work, Healthy Noodes is working for you.

The Recipe

Healthy Noodes with some beef burgers. You know the drill on how to cook this one, burgers in a tray in the over until cooked, you know how to cook the noodes. But, for this dish you'll drain all the water from the noodes before serving, so both noodes and burgers can be served on the plate. Add in some vegetables if you want to impress your mother.

Curry

The Story

Who doesn't love a bit of spice in their lives? With our curry flavour we aim for a medium level of spice, that satisfying burn, and why not share that with a special someone?

The Recipe

Healthy Noodes with a spread of charred vegetables. Heat your grill to medium - high heat, and while that's heating, get to chopping your peppers, onions, and tomatoes. When the grill is ready, place the cut vegetables along with green beans on to the grill. Let them cook for about 10 minutes, turning the vegetables over half way through. You know how to cook the noodes. Whether you leave them in the water they're cooked in or drain them and add vegetables on top. Either way, it'll spice up your night with that special someone.
