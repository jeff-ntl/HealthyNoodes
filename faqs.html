Frequently Asked Questions

How much is Healthy Noodes?

For one packet of Healthy Noodes, you will pay €0.70. We also offer a multi-pack which contains 4 packets of noodles and this costs €2.50. Currently we only offer prices in euros as we are only on sale in Ireland.

What flavours of Healthy Noodes are available?

Currently we only offer chicken flavour, curry flavour, and beef flavour. But the flavour is really up to you, if you're not feeling the flavour in the packet, you can flavour them yourself with whatever spices/flavouring you want! We also recommend if you're looking for a fancy meal for that special someone, add some vegetables and meat to the noodles, they'll be impressed enough to eat with you again!

Where can we buy Healthy Noodes?

You can buy Healthy Noodes in Tesco and Supervalue. We are currently looking in to selling our noodes in Dunnes Stores, Aldi, and Lidl. Check back with us soon for more information!
