Who are we?

We are Anything Healthy, a company based in Waterford, Ireland dedicated to health and convenience. We started out in 2012 making healthy instant pasta as we felt that the instant food world needed to have more healthy options that offered the same convenience as less healthy options. As our company grew, we distributed our product to more stores and increased our staff from 5 to 20 people! Yay for local job development!


Now, 7 years later, we are expanding from instant pasta into instant noodles. What we saw was that people thought that instant noodles are unhealthy, and therefore it was time for Anything Healthy to step in and step up for our customers! And that leads us to where we are now, Healthy Noodes! Yes, we do realise the pun in our name, but that's half the fun, no? Healthy Noodes are here to make your instant noodles experience a healthier and happier one!

Our Mission

Our mission is to let the world know consuming instant noodle can also be healthy.

Our Goal

We aim to be the best instant noodle company in the world.

Our Promise

We promise our customer only bringing the best taste to them.

Our Team

We have professional and motivational employees from different countries.
